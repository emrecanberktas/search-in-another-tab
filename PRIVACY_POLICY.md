# Privacy Policy - Search in Another Tab

**Last Updated**: February 9, 2026

## Overview

Search in Another Tab ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains our data practices.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data.**

The Extension:
- ✅ Does NOT collect personal information
- ✅ Does NOT track browsing history
- ✅ Does NOT use cookies or analytics
- ✅ Does NOT communicate with external servers
- ✅ Does NOT share data with third parties
- ✅ Does NOT store any data locally

## How the Extension Works

The Extension operates entirely locally on your device:

1. Detects when you select text on a webpage
2. Displays a search button near your selection
3. When clicked, opens Google search in a new tab
4. All processing happens locally in your browser

## Permissions Used

The Extension requests only one permission:

### `activeTab`
- **Purpose**: To display the search button on the current webpage
- **Scope**: Only on the active tab you're viewing
- **Data Access**: No data is read or stored

## Third-Party Services

When you click the search button, you are redirected to Google Search. Your interaction with Google is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

The Extension itself does not have any relationship with Google and does not receive any information about your searches.
